About Us
Discover the potential of personalized artistry and boundless possibilities

The Karat Shop story begins in 1976 as a jewelry wholesaler in New York, supplying top quality jewelry to retail stores around the country. Our business grew tremendously, and we decided to sell our beautiful jewelry as a retailer, making it easy for customers to shop. With an eye for fashion and style, we opened a shop in Westbury and in Huntington Station, at our current location. This way, we were able to pass on great savings to our customers on Long Island, New York City and the tri-state area.

Ask Us

Before contacting our support team, we have put together a number of frequently asked questions for you to try and assist you with any queries you might have. If you need further support, please email us at customercare@thekaratshop.com

What are the stores hours?

We are open Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday 11-6, Thursday 11-7, Friday and Saturday 11-6. We are closed on Sundays except in December. We have extended hours for the holidays. Please call the store to inquire.

Do I need an appointment to shop for an engagement ring?

No appointment is necessary. We have diamond specialists who can assist you any time during regular business hours.

Do you repair jewelry on site?

Yes, we have an experienced jeweler on site and most repairs are done right here within a couple of days.

Can you size rings or repair jewelry even if it was purchased elsewhere?

Yes, we repair all your jewelry, even heirloom pieces.

Do you carry natural or lab grown diamonds?

We carry both natural and lab grown diamonds. We also carry precious and semi precious stones. If we dont have the exact stone you desire, we can get it for you.

Can you design unique pieces of jewelry?

We have experienced designers on staff who can help you create a special piece of jewelry

Do you appraise jewelry?

Yes, please call to make an appointment and we will be happy to appraise your jewelry.

Do you repair watches and change watch batteries?

We carry all the popular types of watch batteries and usually can change the battery while you wait. We also offer watch repair services.
